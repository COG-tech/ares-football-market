Formula

ARES_PLAYER_SCORE

ARES Player Score combines position performance, efficiency, role usage, league context, volume availability, durability, trend form, and confidence. Market Score is a separate asset signal, not a transfer fee.

Confidence

Source Coverage + Recency

Confidence is a reliability label. It reflects source coverage, sample size, recency, rights status, metric completeness, and cross-source agreement.
