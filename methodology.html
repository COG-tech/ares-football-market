Example ARES Calculation

Seeded beta midfielder: Performance 26.4 + Efficiency 17.2 + Role 12.0 + League context 11.3 + Volume 7.4 + Durability 4.2 + Trend 3.1 = 81.6 ARES Score.

Example Market Calculation

Market Score combines ARES quality, age curve, position scarcity, league strength, transfer signal, durability, and confidence. A young player can outscore an older star on asset value before becoming the better footballer.

High ARES, Lower Market

A 32-year-old elite midfielder can keep a high ARES Score while Market Score falls because age curve and contract optionality are weaker.

Young High Market

A 20-year-old winger with role expansion can have a high Market Score because upside, scarcity, and transfer signal are strong.

What ARES Does Not Claim

ARES Score is not a transfer fee. ARES Score is not a salary estimate. ARES Score is not a fantasy rank. ARES Score is not a scouting report by itself. Market Score is not the same as market price. Market Score is not a guaranteed sale value. Market Score is not a betting line. Market Score is not an official club valuation.
