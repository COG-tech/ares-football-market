ARES FOOTBALL MARKET - PRODUCT INTENT

What I was trying to build
--------------------------
I was trying to build ARES Football Market into a real football market intelligence product.

The goal was to make it feel like a football version of Transfermarkt plus PFF:

- Transfermarkt-style market boards for players, clubs, leagues, transfers, contract signals, youth assets, risers, fallers, and value movement.
- PFF-style performance scoring with ARES Scores, position-specific components, confidence labels, and clear methodology.
- Global-first football coverage, not only Europe. Asia, MLS, North America, Liga MX, AFC competitions, Japan, Korea, Saudi Arabia, China, India, Australia, and Southeast Asia should be visible early.

The product idea
----------------
ARES should answer two different questions:

1. How good is the player on the field?
   This is the ARES Score.

2. How valuable is the player as a football asset?
   This is the Market Score.

ARES Score is about performance quality.
Market Score is about asset value.

Market Score should consider:

- ARES quality
- age curve
- position scarcity
- league strength
- role security
- contract signal
- transfer movement
- durability
- data confidence

The public product
------------------
The free/public site should not look empty.

It should show enough useful football market structure to make people trust the product before asking them to pay.

Public pages should include:

- homepage market terminal
- player search
- ARES player rankings
- market value rankings
- club portfolio boards
- league strength boards
- Asia market board
- MLS market board
- North America market board
- transfer movement board
- watchlist
- methodology

Every public board should show seeded or real rows, not empty headers.

The premium product
-------------------
The deeper ARES intelligence should be locked for premium users.

Premium should include:

- full ARES component grades
- full Market Score breakdown
- position ranks
- age curve
- transfer value signal
- team fit
- risk score
- movement history
- comparable players
- club strategy boards
- deeper roster analysis

The premium layer should be visible enough that users understand what is locked, but the public layer should still prove value.

Important public beta rule
--------------------------
Until approved live data feeds are connected, the site should use clearly marked seeded public-beta demo data.

Every seeded row should be marked:

data_mode: public_beta_demo

The site should say Public Beta Demo in small trust labels, not use words like placeholder or loading.

What should not happen
----------------------
The site should not show:

- Loading cards
- Pending cards
- fake rows without labels
- placeholder warnings
- empty table headers
- "rows load from file" public copy
- gridiron terms on soccer pages, such as Snap Counts, Depth Chart, or Trade Value

Correct soccer wording:

- Minutes / Role
- Position Usage
- Transfer Value Signal

Image strategy
--------------
I wanted player images, club visuals, and league visuals, but they need to be legally safe.

Do not scrape or copy images from:

- Google Images
- Transfermarkt
- ESPN
- club websites
- MLS pages
- Getty
- Reuters
- AP
- random social media

Safe image options:

- ARES-owned generated player images, club badges, and league badges
- provider-supplied photos only if the provider terms allow public display
- Wikimedia Commons only when the specific file license allows reuse and attribution is stored
- uploaded assets that ARES owns or has permission to use

Current safe beta solution:

- ARES-owned generated player images
- ARES-owned generated club badges
- ARES-owned generated league badges

Why this matters
----------------
The product cannot look like a dead demo.

It needs to feel like a working football market terminal, even before real feeds are connected.

The user should immediately see:

- top ARES players
- top market assets
- young breakout assets
- latest market changes
- latest transfer signals
- club portfolio rankings
- league strength rankings
- Asia market boards
- MLS market boards
- North America market boards

Trust language
--------------
The site should be honest:

- Public Beta Demo means the current rows are seeded fictional records.
- Live football data is not connected yet.
- ARES Score is not a transfer fee.
- Market Score is not the same as market price.
- ARES does not claim to replace scouting.
- ARES does not use restricted commercial scraping.

What still needs to be connected later
--------------------------------------
To become a real database instead of a seeded public beta, ARES still needs approved data sources for:

- real player identity
- real club identity
- real league identity
- real minutes and match stats
- real transfer movement
- real contract signals
- real injuries and availability
- licensed or provider-approved player photos
- licensed or approved club and league logos if real marks are used

Short version
-------------
I was trying to build:

ARES Football Market: a global football asset terminal that combines PFF-style performance grading with Transfermarkt-style market intelligence.

The public site should show credible public-beta boards immediately.
The premium product should lock the deeper ARES intelligence.
The data and images must be legally safe.
The product should feel alive, global, and trustworthy.
